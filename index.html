Country-Specific Immigration Services

Explore migration opportunities across top destinations with comprehensive visa pathways and settlement support

Australia

Points-based skilled migration with strong job market and excellent quality of life. Popular for professionals, students, and families.

  • Skilled Migration - Subclass 189, 190, 491 (Points-based PR)
  • Employer Sponsored - Subclass 482, 186 (Temporary & Permanent)
  • Student Visas - Subclass 500 (Pathway to PR)
  • Family & Business - Partner, Parent, Investor visas
Processing: 6-12 months | PR Path: 2-4 years

Canada

Express Entry system offers fastest PR pathway. Strong economy, multicultural society, and excellent healthcare system.

  • Express Entry - FSW, CEC, FST (Fast-track PR program)
  • Provincial Nominee (PNP) - All provinces (Enhanced chances)
  • Study & Work - Student permits, LMIA work permits
  • Family & Business - Sponsorship, Start-up Visa
Processing: 6-8 months | PR Path: 1-2 years

United Kingdom

Points-based immigration system with excellent education and healthcare. Strong job opportunities in tech, finance, and healthcare.

  • Skilled Worker Visa - Points-based system (PR pathway)
  • Student Visas - Graduate route to work (2-3 years)
  • Health & Care Worker - Fast-track for healthcare professionals
  • Family & Business - Partner visas, Innovator, Start-up visas
Processing: 3-6 months | ILR: 5 years

United States

Diverse visa options for work, study, and family reunification. Strong economy with opportunities in tech, healthcare, and finance.

  • H-1B Work Visa - Specialty occupations (Green Card path)
  • F-1 Student Visa - OPT work authorization (3 years)
  • EB Green Cards - Employment-based permanent residence
  • Family Immigration - Spouse, children, parents sponsorship
Processing: 6-12 months | Green Card: 2-5 years

New Zealand

Beautiful landscapes with work-life balance. Points-based system with opportunities in healthcare, IT, and skilled trades.

  • Skilled Migrant Category - Points-based PR (160+ points)
  • Essential Skills Work - Job offer required (PR pathway)
  • Student Visas - Post-study work rights (3 years)
  • Family & Business - Partner, Parent, Investor visas
Processing: 6-9 months | PR Path: 2-3 years

Europe (EU/Schengen)

Multiple countries with EU Blue Card, Opportunity Card, and national work permits. Strong economy and excellent quality of life.

  • EU Blue Card - Germany, Netherlands, France (High-skilled)
  • Opportunity Card - Germany Chancenkarte (Points-based)
  • National Work Permits - Country-specific programs
  • Student & Family - Study visas, Family reunification
Processing: 3-6 months | PR Path: 3-5 years
